Meet the Revolution

THE CAPTAIN

SERGEI TAKEYOURPANTSOV

  • HEIGHT: 5'9"
  • WEIGHT: 180 LBS
  • POSITION: LEFT WING
  • SHOOTS: RIGHT
  • HOMETOWN: ST. PETERSBURG, RUSSIA

Sergei Takeyourpantsov is a force at both ends of the ice. He uses his exceptional defensive instincts to steal the puck, creating scoring opportunities for his team while his opponents scramble. The Captain uses his elite offensive creativity both to set up his teammates, and to attack the net himself to score. Sergei is consistently ranked among the top 100 players in the world, both for points scored and for winning percentage.

2020-2021 STATS

G: 3,106 A: 2,072 PTS: 5,178 PTS/GP: 3.4

THE HAMMER

AL BONKOVICH

  • HEIGHT: 6'1"
  • WEIGHT: 210 LBS
  • POSITION: CENTRE
  • SHOOTS: LEFT
  • HOMETOWN: SOCHI, RUSSIA

Al "The Bonk" Bonkovich is the Revolution's muscle. She's determined to make her mark by setting up plays out of the faceoff and by clearing paths for her teammates to shoot to score. Defensively you'll find The Bonk following her opponents like a heat-seeking missile, waiting for the perfect opportunity to explode. She is among the most physical players on the stats leaderboard. Al's combination of physicality and finesse make her one of the most interesting centres in the EASHL.

2020-2021 STATS

G: 492 A: 830 PTS: 1,322 PTS/GP: 2.1
