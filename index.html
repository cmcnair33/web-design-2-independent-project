ABOUT US

The Russian Revolution are a competitive ice hockey team within the EA Sports Hockey League (EASHL). The Revolution were founded by Sergei Takeyourpantsov while he was stuck in a COVID-19 lockdown and had nothing to do excpet play video games. Sergei soon recruited some team members: Centre Al Bonkovich, and former Revolutionist Viktor Turndalightsov. The team quickly gained chemistry and went on to win multiple division championships as the Revolution players began to make names for themselves. After dominating the competition in Russia, the Revolution are looking to compete in North America against the best The West has to offer.

OUR ARENA

The Russian Revolution play each and every one of our home games at the luxurious Smirnoff Ice Palace in St. Petersburg, Russia, Sergei's hometown. The facility features both indoor and outdoor arenas, a state-of-the-art gym, a food court featuring gourmet options such as Subway and Booster Juice, and an honest, trustworthy pharmacy that isn't suspicious at all and definitely doesn't deserve the investigations.
