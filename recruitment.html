WHY JOIN?

The Russian Revolution are currently accpeting new prospects. We've had a ton of recent intrest from local players as we make our move to North America, and for good reason. We offer world-class training and encourage all of our players to have a good time on the ice. We aren't "old-school", and we love it when our Revolutionists show off some creativity on the ice. If you're looking for a good team and good fun, The Russian Revolution is right for you!

THE TRYOUT PROCESS

The process in joining the Revolution is very straight forward. If you're interested in becoming a Revolutionist, send us an email at info@revolutionhockey.com and explain why you think you'd be a valuable meber of our team. If our advisors think you're a strong candidate, we will reply to arrange a tryout with you. The tryout consists of five exhibition games, where you will play alongside the Revolution and we will evaluate your performance and make the decision.
